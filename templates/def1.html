Defence Career Roadmap

Step 1: Research & Preparation (6-12 months)

Research different military branches, speak with recruiters, and begin physical training preparation.

Step 2: Application Process (3-6 months)

Complete applications, testing (ASVAB), medical examinations, and background checks.

Step 3: Basic Training (8-12 weeks)

Complete basic military training to learn fundamental skills, discipline, and military customs.

Step 4: Advanced Training (2-6 months)

Receive specialized training in your chosen Military Occupational Specialty (MOS).

Step 5: First Assignment (2-4 years)

Begin your military career with your first assignment, gaining experience and demonstrating leadership potential.

Step 6: Career Advancement (2-5 years)

Pursue advanced training, education, and promotions to advance your military career.
