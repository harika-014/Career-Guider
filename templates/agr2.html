Agriculture offers diverse career opportunities beyond traditional farming. Each field has unique requirements and opportunities.

Plant Genetics Soil Chemistry Pest Management Precision Agriculture

Sustainable Ag

Alternative Pathways to Agriculture Careers

Community College Transfer Pathway

Start at a community college to build foundational skills before transferring to a 4-year program.

  • Hands-on agricultural experience
  • Lower cost for initial coursework
  • Ability to improve GPA before transfer
  • Articulation agreements with universities
Apprenticeships & On-the-Job Training

Gain practical experience while learning essential agricultural skills.

  • Earn while you learn
  • Direct industry experience
  • Networking opportunities
  • Pathway to certification programs
Certificate Programs

Short-term programs focused on specific agricultural skills and knowledge.

  • Focused skill development
  • Quicker entry into workforce
  • Specialized training
  • Stackable credentials
Extension & Outreach Programs

Programs designed to provide agricultural education and training to communities.

  • Community-based learning
  • Practical application focus
  • Networking with professionals
  • Often low-cost or free

Skill Development Roadmap

Step 1: Foundational Skill Assessment (1-2 months)

Identify specific areas of interest and needed skills through self-assessment and career exploration.

Step 2: Basic Skill Development (3-6 months)

Focus on developing basic knowledge in plant science, animal science, and agricultural business principles.

Step 3: Hands-On Experience (6-12 months)

Gain practical experience through internships, volunteering, or entry-level positions in agriculture.

Step 4: Formal Education & Training

Enroll in certificate programs, associate degrees, or bachelor's programs in agricultural fields.

Step 5: Specialization & Certification

Pursue specialized training and certifications in your chosen agricultural field.

Step 6: Professional Development

Continue learning through workshops, conferences, and advanced certifications to advance your career.
