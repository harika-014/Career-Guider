Sports Career Education Roadmap

Step 1: High School Athletic Participation & Education

Participate in school sports, maintain good academic standing, and develop foundational athletic skills and sports knowledge.

Step 2: Undergraduate Degree (2-4 years)

Pursue a Bachelor's degree in Sports Management, Kinesiology, Exercise Science, or related fields. Consider NCAA participation if pursuing athletics.

Step 3: Gain Practical Experience

Complete internships with sports organizations, volunteer at sporting events, or work in entry-level sports positions to build your resume.

Step 4: Professional Certifications (Optional)

Obtain relevant certifications such as Certified Personal Trainer (CPT), Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS), or coaching certifications.

Step 5: Graduate Education (Optional)

Consider a Master's degree in Sports Administration, Sports Medicine, or MBA with sports concentration for advanced positions.

Step 6: Entry into Professional Sports Career

Begin your career in your chosen specialization, continuing to network and develop skills through professional development opportunities.
