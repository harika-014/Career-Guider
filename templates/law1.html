Legal Education Roadmap

Step 1: Undergraduate Degree (4 years)

Complete a bachelor's degree in any field, maintaining a strong GPA. Consider majors that develop critical thinking and writing skills.

Step 2: LSAT Preparation & Exam

Prepare for and take the Law School Admission Test (LSAT), aiming for a competitive score to strengthen your application.

Step 3: Law School (3 years)

Complete a Juris Doctor (JD) program, focusing on building a strong academic record and gaining practical experience through clinics and internships.

Step 4: Bar Examination

Prepare for and pass the bar examination in the state where you wish to practice law.

Step 5: Entry-Level Legal Position

Begin your career as an associate at a law firm, government attorney, public defender, or in-house counsel.

Step 6: Specialization & Advancement (2-5 years)

Gain experience, develop expertise in a specific area of law, and advance to partnership, leadership roles, or specialized practice.
